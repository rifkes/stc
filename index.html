Sadleir Technical Consultancy is a UK‑based independent engineering consultancy practice, established in 1995, serving the construction industry and allied professions across the field of civil engineering. We assist private, commercial and public-sector clients in the realisation of a variety of engineering and maritime transportation projects and in the maintenance of civil engineering installations. Services provided encompass study, analysis and reporting, design, procurement, on-site supervision and project monitoring.

Technical and project management support are offered across a range of engineering disciplines, with specialisation in the areas of:

  • Maritime civil engineering, ports and transportation
  • Appraisal, structural pathology and repair management of reinforced concrete
  • Derelict land rehabilitation and associated surface hydrology

Principal Andrew P.R. Sadleir BSc CEng MICE MASCE ACIArb has some 41 years' professional experience, having worked with leading consulting engineers, specialist contractors, project promoters and funding institutions both in the UK and overseas.

Sadleir Technical Consultancy is based in Cornwall, in the South-West of England. Commissions are undertaken both across the UK and worldwide, and rapid mobilisation may be possible, subject to availability. By virtue of our low overheads, our fee rates are realistic and cost-effective.

We are a small team with specialised knowledge; however, where required for a particular project, we may call upon the expertise of other trusted independent consultants, covering a range of specialised disciplines.
